About The Game

Forza 6 doesn't stop at delivering a heart-pounding racing experience. With a robust online multiplayer mode, you can test your skills against drivers from around the globe. Compete in exhilarating races, join car clubs, and showcase your customized vehicles. The competition is fierce, and only the best will rise to the top.

System Requirements

Before you embark on your high-speed racing adventure in Forza 6, make sure your system meets the following requirements:

  • Operating System: Windows 10
  • Processor: Intel Core i5 3.5GHz or AMD FX-8350
  • Memory: 8 GB RAM
  • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 970 / AMD Radeon R9 290X
  • DirectX: Version 12
  • Storage: 50 GB available space
  • Sound Card: DirectX compatible sound card
  • Additional Notes: Internet connection required for online multiplayer

Mature Content

Please be aware that Forza 6 contains mature content that may not be suitable for all players. The game has been rated for players aged 13 and older.

Forza 6 features realistic depictions of high-speed racing, including intense competition, collisions, and challenging weather conditions. The game may also include mild violence and occasional use of strong language.

Description

Get ready to experience the adrenaline-fueled excitement of Forza 6, a racing masterpiece that pushes the boundaries of realism and entertainment. Immerse yourself in the breathtaking world of high-speed competition, where every turn, every straightaway, and every second counts.
